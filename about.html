History & Culture

Eritrea, located in the Horn of Africa, is a nation of deep history and cultural pride. Asmara, the capital city, is recognized for its preserved Italian-modernist architecture and vibrant street life.

The country's traditions, music, and cuisine reflect influences from Africa, the Middle East, and the Mediterranean, creating a unique national identity.
