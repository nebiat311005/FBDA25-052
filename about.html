About Eritrea

Eritrea, officially the State of Eritrea, is a country in the Horn of Africa. It is bordered by Ethiopia to the south, Sudan to the west, and Djibouti to the southeast. The Red Sea lies to the east.

History

Eritrea has a rich history dating back thousands of years. It was an early center of Christianity and has been influenced by various cultures including Axumite, Ottoman, and Italian colonial rule.

Culture

Eritrean culture is diverse, with nine ethnic groups and several languages. The country is known for its traditional music, dance, and cuisine.

Geography

Eritrea features diverse landscapes from coastal plains to high mountains, including the highest point, Emba Soira at 3,018 meters.
