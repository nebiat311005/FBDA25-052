History of Eritrea

Struggles of the Eritrean People

Eritrea was colonized by Italy, then administered by Britain. In 1962, it was annexed by Ethiopia. This led to a 30-year armed struggle for independence.

Independence

After a long war, Eritrea gained independence in 1993 following a referendum. The Eritrean people showed great resilience and determination during this difficult period.
