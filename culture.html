Eritrean Culture & Traditions

Coffee Ceremony

The coffee ceremony is very important in Eritrea. People roast coffee beans, grind them, and drink together. It is a time for talking and being together.

Traditional Food

Injera with different stews is the main food. People eat together from one big plate. This shows unity and sharing.

Languages and Religion

Eritrea has many languages like Tigrinya, Tigre and Arabic. Both Christianity and Islam are practiced. This makes our culture rich.
