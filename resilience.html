Resilience of the Eritrean People

During the Struggle

Eritrean fighters and civilians showed great strength during the 30-year war. Many women also fought alongside men.

In Daily Life

Even with challenges like sanctions and economic difficulties, Eritreans continue to work hard, support their families, and keep their culture alive.

The Diaspora

Eritreans living abroad send money home, organize cultural events, and help new arrivals. This shows strong community resilience.

Hope for the Future

Despite all difficulties, the Eritrean people remain proud, determined and hopeful for a better future.
