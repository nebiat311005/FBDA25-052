Tourist Destinations in Eritrea

Asmara

Asmara, the capital city, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its Art Deco architecture and vibrant markets.

Massawa

Massawa is a historic port city with Ottoman and Italian influences, featuring beautiful beaches and coral reefs.

Dahlak Islands

An archipelago of islands in the Red Sea, perfect for diving and snorkeling with rich marine life.

Qohaito

An ancient city with rock-hewn churches and monasteries, offering a glimpse into Eritrea's early Christian history.
